Favorite Restaurants

New York is home to thousands of amazing restaurants. Whatever you are in the mood for, there is likely a restaurant that will satisfy that desire very close by. Here is a list of great restaurants in NYC if you are unsure where to go:

Lavo
This restaurant is one of my favorite places to get Italian food. It also doubles as a nightclub which makes it a great place to get dinner and have a fun night. This extensive menu contains basically all the Italian food you could think of and will definitely live up to the expectations. The dessert options include a twenty-layer chocolate cake that definitely will make your mouth water!

TAO
This Asian fusion style restaurant is known for fun foods with a lively atmosphere. They serve a wide variety of Japanese and Chinese foods from handmade sushi rolls to lo mein and friend rice. There also stands a large Buddha in the center of the restaurants that is a focal piece to this dining place.

Vandal
This is one of the most photogenic restaurants in the city with a signature large sculpture of a pink bunny as you enter, while also containing artwork throughout the entire restaurant and an entertaining photo-video booth. They offer a variety of food ranging from delicious mini burgers and a classic hot pretzel steak tartare.

Catch
This globally influenced seafood restaurant is located in the heart of the meatpacking district. Catch incorporates a variety of themes to make their restaurant sophisticated yet approachable. They create modern versions of classic seafood dishes and has a combination of great food, service, and atmosphere.

The Smith
This restaurant is very casual and can be found in a variety of locations throughout the city in East Village, Midtown, Lincoln Center, and NoMad. It is an American Brasserie that is very upbeat letting customers feel the energy the second they step inside. It is open for breakfast, lunch, and dinner that feature selections of bistro plates.
